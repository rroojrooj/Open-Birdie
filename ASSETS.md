# Bundled CC0 Assets

All assets below are CC0 (public domain) unless noted. No attribution is legally
required for CC0; recorded here for provenance.

## HDRIs
- `public/assets/hdri/meadow_4k.hdr` — "Meadow" by Poly Haven (CC0).
  Source: https://polyhaven.com/a/meadow
  Used as the renderer's environment map (image-based lighting), background, and
  ground-projected horizon (Tier 0).

## Foliage cards (Tier 1 trees)
- `public/assets/foliage/fir_twig_diff_1k.png` + `fir_twig_alpha_1k.png` — conifer
  foliage card (diffuse + separate opacity) from "Fir Tree 01" by Poly Haven (CC0).
  Source: https://polyhaven.com/a/fir_tree_01
- `public/assets/foliage/leaves_diff_1k.png` — deciduous foliage card (RGBA, alpha
  embedded) from "Tree Small 02" by Poly Haven (CC0).
  Source: https://polyhaven.com/a/tree_small_02
  Foliage textures only (not the meshes) — used on instanced cross-quad billboards.
